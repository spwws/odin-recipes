Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a popular food item consumed by many around the world. It is generally circular in shape and made of dough with ingredients on top.

Pizza can be baked in traditional charcoal oven or electrical convection ovens.

Ingredients

  • pizza dough
  • tomato slices
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough (From store)
  2. Add tomato slices
  3. Add cheese
  4. Bake in oven
